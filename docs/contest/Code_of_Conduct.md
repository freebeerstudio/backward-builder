# Participant Code of Conduct Agreement

By registering for and participating in this vibeathon, I acknowledge and agree to the following:

## 1. Respect & Inclusion
- I will treat all participants, mentors, judges, sponsors, and organizers with respect.
- I will help maintain a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment.

## 2. Collaboration & Integrity
- I will work in good faith, avoid plagiarism, and represent my work honestly.
- I will follow event rules, timelines, and organizer instructions.

## 3. Event Safety & Conduct
- I will follow all venue rules and safety instructions.
- I will conduct myself in a professional and respectful manner throughout the event.

## 4. Consequences
- I understand that violation of this Code of Conduct may result in removal from the event and disqualification from awards or future participation.
