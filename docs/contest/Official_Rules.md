- Employees, representatives and agents of such organizations, and all members of their immediate family or household.
- Any Judge (defined below), or company or individual that employs a Judge.
- Any parent company, subsidiary, or other affiliate of any organization described above.
- Any other individual or organization whose participation in the Vibeathon would create, in the sole discretion of the Sponsor and/or Administrator, a real or apparent conflict of interest.

## SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

### How to Enter
1. Register at https://vibeathon.us by clicking "Register Now" and completing all required fields.
2. Build and submit a vibe coded prototype of a software application designed to solve the industry presented problem.
3. Use your platform of choice. You are responsible for any platform-related expenses.
4. Submit by (1) uploading a ZIP file of source code (under 100MB) and (2) providing a publicly accessible live URL through app.vibeathon.us.

### Submission Requirements

- **What to Create:** A vibe coded prototype solving the industry presented problem. Each Entrant may submit one Submission.
- **Project Description:** Text description explaining features and functionality.
- **Live Application URL:** Publicly accessible, no authentication or special configuration required, must stay live through judging.
- **Visual Representation / Demo Video:**
  - Less than 5 minutes
  - Comprehensive demonstration of features and functionality
  - Uploaded as MP4 or MOV, under 100MB, through app.vibeathon.us
  - No third party trademarks or copyrighted music without permission
- **Testing:** Application must be available free of charge for testing/evaluation during the Vibeathon and through judging.
- **Original Work:** Must be your original work, solely owned by you.
- **Third Party Data & Tools:** May use public data sources and integrate SDKs/APIs if authorized.
- **Multiple Submissions:** Allowed, but each must be unique and substantially different.
- **Language:** All materials must be in English (or include English translations).

## JUDGING AND CRITERIA

### Judges
Evaluated primarily by an AI judging system, with potential human judge oversight.

### Scoring

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Impact & Relevance | 40% | How effectively the Application addresses the industry problem |
| Feasibility | 15% | How readily the Application can be piloted to a real use application |
| Innovation | 15% | Creativity and originality; strong use of AI |
| User Experience | 10% | Clarity and accessibility to the end user |
| Demo Quality | 20% | Problem description, solution explanation, technology used, real-world connection, reliability |

### Finalists
Top 5 Entrants' video submissions will be shown as winners are revealed.

### Tie Breaker
Highest score in the first applicable criterion wins. Process repeats through subsequent criteria as needed.
