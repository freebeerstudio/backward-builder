# Startup Bring-Your-Own Challenge

## Problem #1

Bring your own startup idea and vibe-code it into a working MVP in under a week. This challenge is for founders, aspiring entrepreneurs, and builders who have a problem they want to solve — whether it's a brand-new idea or something you've been thinking about for years.

You'll use AI-powered coding tools to rapidly prototype a real product. Judges will evaluate your submission on impact, feasibility, innovation, demo quality, and user experience.
