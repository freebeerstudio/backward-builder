## Backward Builder

**Backward Builder is an AI-powered curriculum design tool that lets teachers go from an enduring understanding to a complete, deployable unit plan — with assessments, auto-graded quizzes, and aligned learning activities — in minutes instead of hours.**

### The Problem

Every trained teacher knows backward design (Understanding by Design / UbD): start with what students should understand, design assessments that prove understanding, then plan instruction that gets students there. It's the gold standard of curriculum planning, taught in every education program in the country.

But the tools teachers actually have are Word documents, blank planning templates, and 45 minutes of prep time they're already spending on grading, emails, and parent calls. The person closest to the curriculum problem — the classroom teacher — has always known what great instruction looks like. They've just been locked out of building the digital tools that bring it to life.

### The Solution

Backward Builder guides teachers through the UbD framework using a conversational AI-powered flow:

1. **Start with the End** — The teacher enters an enduring understanding (the big idea students should walk away with). The AI helps refine and sharpen it if needed.

2. **Design the Assessment** — The AI generates assessment options aligned to that understanding. The teacher selects, modifies, or combines them — bringing their professional judgment to bear without starting from a blank page.

3. **Deploy a Live Quiz** — Backward Builder generates a ready-to-use, deployable quiz with auto-grading. Students can take it immediately. Results flow into a dashboard the teacher can review in real time — no manual grading, no spreadsheet wrangling.

4. **Build the Learning Path** — The AI produces a sequenced set of learning activities designed to move students from where they are to where the assessment says they need to be. Every activity is aligned backward from the assessment, just like UbD demands.

### Who It's For

Middle school and high school teachers who understand backward design but don't have the time or technical skill to build digital assessments, quizzes, and aligned unit plans from scratch. Backward Builder treats teachers as the curriculum experts they are — and handles the building for them.

### Technology

- AI-powered conversational flow using Claude for curriculum generation, assessment alignment, and activity sequencing
- Live quiz deployment with auto-grading and a real-time results dashboard
- Built with a modern web stack, designed for simplicity and speed
- No login required for students taking quizzes — frictionless classroom deployment

### Why It Matters

There are 3.7 million teachers in the United States. Every one of them plans curriculum. Most do it alone, with generic templates, under severe time pressure. Backward Builder doesn't replace teacher expertise — it amplifies it. The teacher provides the domain knowledge (what students need to understand), and the AI handles the construction (assessments, quizzes, activities). 

This is the "Vibe the Gap" challenge in action: the person closest to the problem finally has the power to build the solution — no developer required.

### About the Builder

Backward Builder was created by a former middle school teacher who went on to work in edtech, school administration, and enterprise technology before becoming an AI developer. This tool exists because its creator spent years watching brilliant teachers struggle with tools that were never designed for how they actually think about curriculum. Backward Builder is the tool they wished they'd had in the classroom.
